Privacy Policy

At 8EEN STORE, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Last updated: October 15, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information when you visit and shop on our website. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Identification Details: Your full name, email address, shipping and billing addresses, phone number, and payment details.
  • Account and Authentication Data: Managed securely through Firebase Authentication to ensure your account security.
  • Usage Data: Including browsing behavior, cookies, and analytics to improve website performance and user experience.
  • Voluntary Information: Any other information you voluntarily provide while interacting with our site or customer service.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders, including shipping and payment processing
  • To manage your account and provide authentication services via Firebase
  • To communicate with you about your orders, promotions, and updates
  • To improve our website, personalize your shopping experience, and detect fraudulent activities
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

Authentication and Data Security

We use Firebase Authentication to securely manage your login credentials and protect your account. Your authentication data is processed under Firebase's privacy and security policies.

All personal information you provide is stored securely in our MongoDB databases with robust encryption and access controls to prevent unauthorized access.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather analytics. These technologies help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Improve website functionality and performance
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information with trusted third parties solely to fulfill your orders and improve our services:

  • Payment Processors: To process your payments securely
  • Shipping Partners: To deliver your orders
  • Service Providers: To maintain and improve our website
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data we hold
  • Update: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
